While it mentions recycling as the current solution, there is potential for a deeper look into the issue. Media can have different biases depending on the type, so it is important to approach the topic without any bias.

To delve deeper into the issue, we need to examine the beginning of the production path. Every tool goes through a path from design to quality control and supply to the market, which must adhere to international standards. If world powers used their influence to encourage manufacturers to produce better-quality products, the products would have a longer lifespan. What Is a Digital Immune System and Why Does It Matter?

Article link - by Lori Perri - October 25, 2022

This article was about how a digital immune system is important, and how owners and users can benefit from it.

A robust digital immune system protects applications and services from anomalies, such as the effects of software bugs or security issues by making applications more resilient so that they recover quickly from failures. It can reduce business continuity risks created when critical applications and services are severely compromised or stop working altogether.

As the writer mentioned, "A digital immune system combines practices and technologies to increase the resilience of products, services and systems."
